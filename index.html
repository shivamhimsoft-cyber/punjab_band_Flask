About Us

Celebrating Punjabi Tradition Since 1986

Welcome to Punjab Band, the premier wedding band service in Balongi, Mohali. We bring the vibrant energy and rich cultural heritage of Punjab to your special celebrations. With our skilled performers, traditional music, and professional arrangements, we ensure your wedding becomes a memorable celebration for everyone.

Rooted in Punjabi traditions since 1986, our team brings decades of expertise to create unforgettable wedding experiences. As Chandigarh's No.1 Band, we take pride in preserving our cultural heritage while adding modern touches that appeal to all generations.

Based near Dashmesh Market in Balongi, Mohali, we serve weddings across Punjab and neighboring states. Our comprehensive services cover everything from the bridegroom's entry to the final ceremonies, ensuring a seamless celebration.

Our Services

Brass Band

Professional musicians in smart uniforms performing traditional and modern wedding tunes to energize your celebrations.

Wedding Horse (Ghodi)

Beautifully decorated white horse for the bridegroom's procession adorned with traditional Punjabi ornaments.

Decorative Rath

Exquisitely decorated carriage with fresh flowers and lights for a royal entry during wedding ceremonies.

Vintage Car

Classic vintage car for an elegant entry that adds a touch of nostalgia to your special day.

Crystal Chhattar

Ornamental canopy with sparkling crystals for a grand entry during night functions and special ceremonies.

Dhol & Foji Band

Authentic Punjabi Dhol players and military-style bands for energetic performances that create festive atmosphere.

Jaago Dhol

Traditional Punjabi Jaago performance with dhol and lights for the pre-wedding festivities.

Giddha Performers

Professional Malwai Giddha dancers in colorful attire to entertain your guests with Punjabi folk dances.

Victoria Rath

Royal Victorian-style carriage decorated with fresh flowers for a majestic wedding procession.

Well-Dressed Performers

Professional artists in traditional Punjabi attire to enhance the visual splendor of your wedding.

Power Generators

Uninterrupted power supply solutions to ensure your wedding festivities continue without any interruption.

Catering Services

Traditional Punjabi catering with a variety of menu options to satisfy your guests' palates.
